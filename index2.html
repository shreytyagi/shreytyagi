Shrey Tyagi

Shrey Tyagi is a Software Engineer and currently works in Infosys (Chandigarh, India). He finished his Bachelors of Engineering in CSE (Computer Science and Engineering) from Chitkara University (Punjab, India) in 2020.

Early Life

Shrey was born in Uttar Pradesh, India to a Hindi-speaking Hindu family. His mother Vibha is a homemaker and father Kapil, worked as an Airman in the Indian Air Force. A few weeks after birth, he was taken to Jorhat (Assam, India) where his father was posted at that time. He spent his infancy there as a hyperactive, extrovert and talkative child. He was fond of toys and watching cartoons on TV. The family of 3 used to visit Saharanpur twice an year to meet his grandparents. The wonderful 2 days long train journeys from Assam to Uttar Pradesh are some of the best memories he still cherishes.
