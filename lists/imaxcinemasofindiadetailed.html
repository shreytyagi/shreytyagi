IMAX Cinemas of India - Detailed

This is a detailed list of all IMAXs of India along with their projection type. For a basic list, click here.

Important points:

  • GT means Grand Theatre and these venues have 1.43:1 ratio. Non-GT venues have a 1.90:1 ratio.
  • Commercial Laser is the highest quality IMAX projectors.
  • Dual Xenon are the worst quality IMAX projectors.
  • 5.1, 6.1, 12.1 denote the sound system configuration.
