Flat Screens of India

This is a list of cinema screens which have an aspect ratio of 1.90:1 or 1.85:1 (called flat). Such screens are special because majority of the screens in India have an aspect ratio or 2.35:1 or 2.39:1 (scope).

Such flat screens are suitable for watching movies delivered in a 1.85:1 or 1.90:1 DCP to cinemas with either content matching the container ratio or other unusal ratios like 1.19:1, 1.33:1, 1.37:1, 1.66:1, 1.78:1, 2.00:1 or similar non-ultrawide ratios.
