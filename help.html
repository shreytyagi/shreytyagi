Help

Please use the header bar in desktop mode or the menu button in phone/tablet mode to exlore the available sections on the websites or external links. The website is divided into 3 major sections: Home Pages, Articles/Blogs, Lists and Photos. There is one main home page that can be accessed using shreytyagi.com or clicking on Shrey Tyagi on top left of the header. On this main page you'll find clickable tiles of all published content. You can find sectionally filtered tiles on their dedicated pages like Articles or Blogs which are basic text based pages. However, Lists and Photos pages are dynamic. In Lists you can see tabular data which is sortable by clicking on any table header of teh column. Click once to sort one way, click again to sort the other way, click again to reset the sort to default order. Lastly, in photos page, you can click any photo to see full screen, you can use next, previous and close buttons just like a slideshow.
