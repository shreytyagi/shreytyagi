Superman Hindi dub vs English comparison

Shrey Tyagi • 13 Jul, 2025

I’ve seen both versions of Superman now, and honestly, the Hindi dub holds up really well. In fact, some lines even felt better written in Hindi than the original English ones.

  • That viral “Chhoti bacchi ho kya” line happens in a scene where characters are naming punk rock bands. Since most Indians wouldn’t get those US band references, the dub replaced them with Desi movie names like Baaghi and Heropanti. It actually worked and felt fun, not cringe.
  • “Brain beats brawn” becomes “Akal sabse balwaan,” which fits perfectly in tone and rhythm.
  • In a scene where Superman is lying down exhausted, Mr. Terrific says “This ain’t playtime” in English, which felt flat. In Hindi, “Apne gaon ki khatiya samajh ke lete hai kya?” landed funnier and more desi.
  • “I make mistakes, and that makes me human” becomes “Haan mujhse galtiyan hoti hain, lekin galtiyaan insaano se hi hoti hain,” which hit harder and felt more sincere.

All in all, I was fully entertained by the Hindi version. After watching both, I actually prefer the Hindi dub.
