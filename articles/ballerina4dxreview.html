Ballerina 4DX and Movie Review

Shrey Tyagi • 14 Jun, 2025

I’ve seen all #JohnWick films but only vaguely remembered the story… so maybe why I couldn’t connect much. Felt like a bland Bollywood-style revenge drama.

Action/fights were solid. Sound mix was front-heavy except during action… great directional effects (gunshots, voices moving in surround).

Hindi dub was fine, except for two poorly voiced kids.

4DX was thrilling: blasts, hits, throws synced perfectly with motion seats. They timed it in a way that they slowly dip the seats (without letting us know) before major impacts, and then used that for a sharp bounce & tilt back... this was really exciting and masterfully done. Lightning, snow (used thrice), wind & smoke added well.

Recommended in 4DX? Yes, but not strongly. I feel it could be better on a bigger screen with deep bass.
