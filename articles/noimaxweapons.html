No IMAX for Weapons

Shrey Tyagi • 1 Aug, 2025

I’ve received official confirmation that Weapons will NOT be released in IMAX in India. With 100% on Rotten Tomatoes and critics calling it the most original and terrifying horror movie in years, it’s unfortunate that Indian audiences won’t get to experience it in the best way. I understand this news may disappoint many, but the decision stems from factors beyond anyone’s control. Certain practical constraints have made an IMAX release unfeasible in India. That said, do seek it out at premium non-IMAX 4K Atmos venues or the best possible screen you can find!
